Welcome to ChicCanvas!

ChicCanvas is an online fashion & lifestyle destination where editors-in-chief Victoria & Elizabeth curate the best in fashion space. This hub is for all fashion lovers, you can find out about upcoming fashion launches and get a first look at Kenya's rising fashion models. Read, shop, get inspired, and subscribe to our newsletter.

In 2023, ChicCanvas was launched exclusively in Nairobi, Kenya. The collection includes apparel (regular, plus, petite) Kenyan models, Upcoming Kenyan Fashion Brands and affordable thrift shops where you can pop-up by and shop!
