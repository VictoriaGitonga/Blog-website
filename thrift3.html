Who Are They?

Seven Sunday Second Hand Clothes, the best place to go shopping where you'll find all kinds of outfits at an affordable price, take a visit.

 

The trendy styles in Seven Sunday shop outfits will leave you looking adorable and having confidence in yourself from head to toe.come shop with seven Sunday to improve your confidence.

 

When it comes to clothes, shoes, bags, carpets, duvets, bedding's, kids clothing, and skates, we are your best choice, pocket wise, location and with a great taste in fashion. Visit Seven Sunday Second hand clothes shop everyday 9am to 8pm.

 

Get the glitz and glam of your wardrobe without breaking the bank. Our designs are high end and will have you as the talk of the town. Prices on all clothing are at maximum Ksh200, children shoes maximum Ksh200, men's shoes and bedding curtains at a maximum of Ksh300.

 

Considering that these are used garments, this store sells high-quality clothing. The clothing is sent fully cleaned and laundered. On open days, when there is a wide selection and fresh merchandise, more people choose to visit.
