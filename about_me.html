About Me

Hi there! ChicCanvas came to me as a creative outlet and a little escape from the coding world. With a knack for finding good clothes and putting outfits together on a small budget, I have learned how to make just about anything chic! My hope for ChicCanvas is that it serves as a source of inspiration for any girl looking to add a little something extra to their everyday outfit or dare to try something new. While here, you can bet to see everything fashion that I find inspiring, motivating, or beautiful.

 

As for me, I am a coffee and wine addict, foodie (brunch is my fav!), and totally and completely fashion-obsessed. While my passion for fashion is undeniable, I am happiest when I am exploring new destinations, and more importantly relaxing with a glass of wine!

 

I hope that ChicCanvas feels like you are chatting with an old friend, through a blog! I am so glad that you found your way here and hope that you stay awhile! Thanks for stopping by!
